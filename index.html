Monty Hall Game!

The Monty Hall game involves three doors, one hiding a prize (e.g., a car) and the other two hiding goats. You begin by selecting one door. Then, the host, who knows where the prize is, opens one of the two remaining doors to reveal a goat. At this point, you are given the option to either stick with your original choice or switch to the other unopened door.

Make your choice!
